Data Analytics Links

November 27, 2021 One-minute read

Some of the most useful courses online

  • The Data Science Course 2021: Complete Data Science Bootcamp. The course is very precise and well organized. They have a good combination of theory with good practical examples tht are relevent today

  • Principles, Statistical and Computational Tools for Reproducible Data Science Harvard also has a very nice Master’s program in Data Science

  • Berkeley Online Data Analytics Boot Camp : Berkeley also has a one year Master’s program on Analytics and Data Science. Have found the Berkeley courses to be good in terms of practicality.

Competitions

  • Kaggle : A very useful source for experimental datasets to play and see how some of the algorithms function. They have a good collection of courses, datasets and competition results.
